# OfferFu Three-Call Script

## Context

This is the three-call script for selling OfferFu itself — the value offer generator sold as a lead magnet. The prospect is any small business owner. The free tool proves you understand the first call; the discovery call proves you understand their business; the ask is implementation.

The upsell isn't "more scripts" — it's booking a discovery call where we identify which of these solves their real problem:
1. **Database reactivation via SMS** — reaching old leads/customers
2. **Outbound telemarketing campaign** — targeted cold/warm calling
3. **AI receptionist & concierge** — answers calls, books appointments 24/7
4. **Delivery automation** — proposals, follow-ups, invoicing, scheduling

---

## Call 1 — The Value Call

**Opener:** "Hey [name], I'm [you] with OfferFu. I built a free tool that creates custom sales scripts for businesses, and I wanted to make sure you knew about it. Can I take 90 seconds?"

**Body:** "We built this AI tool that generates a complete three-call script — opener, value offer, objection handles — for any business in about 5 minutes. It's free to try. Most businesses using it are in [2-3 verticals], but it works for anything where you need to get past the gatekeeper and into a real conversation. I thought of you because [specific reason]."

**Close:** "Here's what I'd like to do: I'll send you a link, you punch in what you sell and who you sell it to, and you'll get a complete script in a couple minutes. No credit card, no signup for the first one. I'll follow up in a few days to see if it was useful — and if it was, I'd love to book 15 minutes to learn about your business and see if we can help beyond just scripts. Sound fair enough?"

**Objection handles:**
- *"Is this a scam?"* — "I get it. The script tool is genuinely free — we give it away because it's the best way to show you we know what we're doing."
- *"I don't do cold calling"* — "The script works for warm leads too — first call to a referral, or follow-up after a networking event."
- *"I already have a script"* — "Compare it. If yours is better, keep using it. This costs nothing to check."

---

## Call 2 — The Follow-Up (2-3 days later)

**Opener:** "Hey [name], [you] from OfferFu. Did you get a chance to try that script generator?"

**Body:** "What I'm finding is that most businesses we talk to fall into one of two camps. Either they need more customers — or they're doing okay on volume but drowning in repetitive work. Which camp are you in?"

*(Listen. Then match:)*

**If more customers:** "For businesses that need more customers, we usually find the fastest win is one of these: **reactivating your old leads and past customers via SMS** — they already know you, they just need a reason to come back. Or running a **targeted outbound campaign** with a script like the one you just generated — but dialed in to your exact list. Have you got a database of past customers or leads you're not talking to?"

**If less busy / drowning in work:** "For businesses that are busy but stretched thin, it's usually **an AI receptionist that books appointments for you while you're on the job**, or **automating a piece of your delivery** — proposals, follow-ups, scheduling, invoicing — whatever eats your day. What's the one thing you wish you didn't have to do every week?"

**Close:** "Here's what I'd like to propose: book 15 minutes with me — not a sales call, a discovery call. I'll ask you about your business, and if I see something we can automate or a campaign we can run that would actually move the needle, I'll show you exactly what it looks like. If not, you keep the free script and we part friends. What's this week looking like for you?"

---

## Call 3 — The Ask (after the discovery call)

**Opener:** "Hey [name], thanks for the time earlier this week. Based on what you told me, here's what I think makes sense."

**Body:** *(Pick one based on discovery call)*

**Option A — Database Reactivation:** "You told me you've got [X] past customers you haven't talked to in [timeframe]. We can set up an SMS campaign that reaches out to them with a value-first offer — not 'buy now,' but 'here's something useful.' We write the script, set up the SMS system, and you just handle the calls that come back. The script tool you tried is the starting point — this is the full engine."

**Option B — Outbound Campaign:** "You've got a clear target market and a good offer. We build a list, craft the script from what you generated, and run a 30-day outbound telemarketing campaign. You close the deals, we handle the dialing and the scripting."

**Option C — AI Receptionist / Concierge:** "You said [specific pain point — missing calls, double-booked, can't get back to people fast enough]. We set up an AI receptionist that answers your calls, books appointments, and sends you the details. It works 24/7, never takes a sick day, and costs a fraction of a hire."

**Option D — Delivery Automation:** "You told me [specific repetitive task]. We can automate that — proposals go out on time, follow-ups happen without you thinking about it, invoices get sent when they should. You stop being the bottleneck."

**Close:** "All of these start with what you already saw — the free script generator proves we understand the first call. The discovery call proved we understand *your* business. Now it's just: do you want us to build it? [Pause. Let them answer.]"

---

## Between-Call Touchpoints

- **After Call 1:** Text the link + one-line email: "Here's that script generator — [link]. Punch in what you sell, get a script in 2 min."
- **After Call 2:** Send a relevant case study or article: "Saw this and thought of our conversation — [link]."
- **After Call 3:** Follow-up text: "Great chatting today. I'll send over [proposal/next steps] by [day]."

---

## Key Principle

The free tool proves you understand the first call. The discovery call proves you understand their business. The ask is implementation, not more scripts.
